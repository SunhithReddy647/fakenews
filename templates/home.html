The Dangers of Fake News

Erodes Trust

Fake news undermines trust in legitimate news sources and institutions, making it difficult for the public to stay informed.

Polarizes Society

By spreading biased and false information, fake news can deepen social and political divisions.

Harms Individuals

Misinformation can lead to harmful decisions, from financial scams to health risks.
