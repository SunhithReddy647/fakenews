The Impact of Fake News

Understanding the consequences of misinformation helps us stay vigilant and informed.

⚠

Erodes Trust

Fake news undermines trust in legitimate news sources and institutions, making it increasingly difficult for the public to stay accurately informed and distinguish credible journalism.

⚖

Polarizes Society

By spreading biased and false information, fake news amplifies social and political divisions, creating echo chambers and hindering constructive dialogue.

⛔

Harms Individuals

Misinformation can lead to harmful decisions affecting health, finances, and safety, from medical scams to investment fraud and beyond.
